POSITIVE DISCIPLINEMURCIA EVENT 2020

This two-day interactive workshop is designed for early childhood educators, teachers, parents, and caregivers who work with children from birth to six years of age. Participants will learn to apply the principles and tools of Positive Discipline, a research-based Adlerian philosophy, to work with groups of young children. They will also gain a deeper understanding of how young children learn, the importance of belonging and social and emotional learning, and the best way to teach those skills in a classroom or caring environment.
